Manufacturing

Throughout my time on UT Austin's FSAE Electric team, I've learned about the manufacturing process. I have machined several components on the car, many of which are shown below.

I machined the parts above primarily on manual machines (lathe and mill). The cone spacers, tapped plugs, ARB connectors, and ARB blades were done entirely on manual machines (I also heat treated the ARB blade). The front and rear bellcranks were made on a CNC mill (I assisted in running the machine for these parts). The assemblies include all of the individual components machined above.
