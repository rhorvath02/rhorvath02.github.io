Vehicle Dynamics - Steering Design

Overall Approach:

The steering system is the driver's interface with the tires. Therefore, we need to design a system that maintains functionality through the additional DOF. I've helped maintain consistency by minimizing bump steer before doing any additional work (I'll add a 2D sketch of the kinematics at a later date). Now we need to focus on our primary design factors: maximizing driver comfort, maximizing the grip of the tires, and miantaining structural integrity.
